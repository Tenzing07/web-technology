CEO of IBM

Thomas john watson Sr was an american businessman who served as the chairman and ceo of IBM. he was born on february 17, 1874. watson developed IBM's management style and corporate culture from john henry patterson's training at NCR(Non cash register).he was oone of the richest men of his time and was called the world's greatest salesman when he died in 1956.
